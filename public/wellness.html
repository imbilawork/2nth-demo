Why a standalone domain (not under biz)?
Clinical requirements
PAR-Q screening, contraindication filtering, SOAP note format, and HPCSA scope of practice rules don't exist in any other domain.
Sensitive health data
POPIA Section 26 classifies health information as special personal information. Requires explicit consent flows and encrypted storage not needed in general biz skills.
Regulated practitioners
Dietitians (HPCSA/ADSA), biokineticists (ESSA), physiotherapists — all have scope of practice limits the AI must respect. The skill layer enforces this by design.
