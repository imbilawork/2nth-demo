The Opportunity
Science-Led Coaching, AI-Amplified
SpinScience already delivers structured, data-driven cycling coaching to athletes across South Africa. The next step is embedding AI agents into that coaching workflow — automating the analytical heavy lifting so Léré can focus more time on athletes and less on spreadsheets. Training plan generation, post-ride analysis, FTP tracking, and periodization scheduling, all running on a purpose-built AI coaching platform powered by 2nth.ai.
📊
Today's Reality
Ride data from Strava, Garmin, and Wahoo arrives in separate silos. Generating personalised training plans and post-ride analysis takes manual effort that scales poorly as athlete numbers grow.
⚡
The AI Shift
An AI coach that ingests ride data automatically, computes TSS, updates the PMC, prescribes next week's sessions, and delivers athlete-facing feedback — at scale, instantly, in plain English.
🇿🇦
Built for South Africa
Highveld altitude adjustments, Cape Town Cycle Tour periodization, Wines2Whales stage race prep, Joberg2C planning — the AI knows SA cycling. It speaks your athletes' language.
🏋️
Athlete Self-Service
Athletes query their own data — "How was my zone distribution last week?" "Am I on track for Epic?" — without needing a coach session for every question. Léré's time is reserved for the conversations that matter.
